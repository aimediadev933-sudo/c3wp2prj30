Project Overview

This comprehensive project assessed Asi@Connect's capacity development and NREN services across seven least developed Asian countries through systematic surveys, field visits, and stakeholder consultations. The initiative aimed to identify infrastructure gaps, training needs, and strategic improvements to enhance regional research and education networking capabilities.

Activities Conducted

Website Development

Created a comprehensive web-based survey system at nrensurvey.bdren.net.bd to collect data from NREN professionals across participating countries.

Baseline Survey

Conducted extensive online survey with responses from 124 institutions to evaluate existing NREN services and training impacts.

Focus Group Discussions

Organized FGDs at 15 universities in Bangladesh to gather qualitative insights on network performance and management needs.

Campus Network Visits

Conducted on-site assessments to observe infrastructure, security systems, and REN services in participating institutions.

Blended Conference

Held final stakeholder conference in Dhaka to present survey findings and discuss improvement recommendations.

Final Report

Compiled comprehensive final report with survey results, FGD findings, and strategic recommendations for future programs.
